About Us

Our Story

It all started with a group of passionate volleyball enthusiasts in Buenos Aires who were tired of struggling to find local tournaments and matches. Inspired by their shared love for the sport, they decided to create a central platform where players, coaches, and fans could connect. Thus, the Buenos Aires Volleyball Center was born. The founders envisioned a dynamic, user-friendly website that would not only list upcoming events but also grow into a bustling online community, uniting volleyball lovers across the city. Today, the BAVC stands as a testament to the power of community, dedication, and a shared passion for the game, continually driving the sport forward in Argentina's vibrant capital.
