Discover Lomas de Zamora!

Lomas de Zamora, located south of the City of Buenos Aires and within the metropolitan area of Greater Buenos Aires, is a city in the province of Buenos Aires, Argentina. It is the capital of the Lomas de Zamora District and has a population of 111,897 inhabitants.

Chamber History

The Chamber of Commerce in Lomas de Zamora has been championing local businesses since 1932. They promote economic growth, offer professional services, and advocate for the business community. Their mission is to drive local prosperity through collaboration, innovation, and support, ensuring businesses thrive in a dynamic and competitive marketplace.
